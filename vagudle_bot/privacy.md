# Privacy Policy for Vagudle Bot

**Last Updated:** June 4, 2026

Thank you for using **Vagudle Bot**. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when the Bot interacts with you, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

By interacting with Vagudle Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

> Vagudle Bot works alongside **Tajin Helper**. For information about how duel game data, leaderboard records, and challenge data are stored and processed, please refer to [Tajin Helper's Privacy Policy](https://king-tajin.dev).

---

## 1. Data We Collect & How We Use It

### Direct Messages
Vagudle Bot's sole function is to deliver Direct Messages to Discord users on behalf of Tajin Helper. To do this, the Bot processes the following:

* **Discord User ID:** Used to open a DM channel and deliver the message. It is not stored by the Bot itself beyond the duration of the request.
* **Message Content:** The content of DMs sent through Vagudle Bot (such as duel results and duel expiry notifications) is generated by Tajin Helper and passed to the Bot for delivery. It is not independently stored or logged by Vagudle Bot.

### Temporary Activity Data
When a Vagudle Activity duel is launched, a short-lived payload containing the following fields is stored in Cloudflare KV:

* Discord User ID
* Duel identifier
* Word, word length, dictionary type, and guess limit
* Timestamp of creation

This data exists solely to load the duel inside the Discord Activity and is **automatically deleted after 24 hours** with no manual intervention required. It is never used for analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the active game session.

### Temporary Logs
To maintain performance and debug errors, the Bot utilizes temporary operational logging:

* Basic request metadata (User IDs, delivery status) may be transiently logged to assist with debugging failed DM deliveries.
* **Retention:** These logs are transient and are automatically rotated out. They are not used to build long-term user profiles.

---

## 2. Infrastructure & Data Security

* **Network Infrastructure:** All webhook traffic to and from Vagudle Bot is routed through **Cloudflare Workers**, protected by HMAC-SHA256 request signing to ensure only authorized systems can trigger the Bot.
* **Temporary Data Storage:** Short-lived activity duel payloads are stored in **Cloudflare KV** with a 24-hour TTL.
* **Log Storage:** All temporary operational logs are stored securely on a dedicated, private server managed directly by the developer.
* **Data Sharing:** We **never** sell, trade, or share any user data or message content with third-party advertisers or external entities. Cloudflare is used solely as infrastructure and is subject to [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

---

## 3. Data Deletion & User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with you.

* **Temporary Logs:** To request immediate purging of any transient logs associated with your Discord User ID, please email **developer@king-tajin.dev**.
* **Activity Duel Data:** Temporary KV data expires automatically after 24 hours. If you need it removed sooner, email **developer@king-tajin.dev** with your Discord User ID and approximate time of the session.
* **Duel Results & Leaderboard Data:** These are managed by Tajin Helper, not Vagudle Bot. Please refer to [Tajin Helper's Privacy Policy](https://king-tajin.dev) for deletion requests relating to persistent game data.
* **Processing:** All deletion requests are processed promptly.
